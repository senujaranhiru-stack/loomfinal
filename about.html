Discover the story behind Sri Lanka's most cherished lifestyle brand, where centuries-old craftsmanship meets contemporary design.

Our Story

Loom & Lane is a well-known Sri Lankan lifestyle brand that has become synonymous with authentic craftsmanship and timeless elegance. Specializing in exquisite home decoration, handcrafted wooden crafts, and fine porcelain, we have dedicated ourselves to preserving and promoting the rich artistic heritage of Ceylon. Our carefully curated collections showcase the extraordinary talent of Sri Lankan artisans who bring centuries-old techniques to life in every piece they create.

With physical outlets strategically located in Colombo, Galle, Kandy, Kalutara, and Anuradhapura, Loom & Lane has established a strong presence across the island. Each showroom offers customers an immersive experience where they can touch, feel, and appreciate the intricate details of our handcrafted treasures. Our commitment to authenticity and quality has earned us the trust of thousands of customers who seek genuine Sri Lankan artistry for their homes and spaces.

Our Mission

Expanding Internationally

Our vision extends far beyond the shores of Sri Lanka. We are committed to expanding internationally, bringing the beauty and authenticity of Sri Lankan craftsmanship to discerning customers around the world. Through strategic partnerships and thoughtful expansion, we aim to establish Loom & Lane as a global ambassador of Ceylon's artistic heritage.

Preserving Artistic Craftsmanship

While we embrace growth and innovation, we remain steadfast in our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of artistic craftsmanship. Every piece that bears the Loom & Lane name is a testament to the skill, passion, and dedication of our artisans. We work closely with master craftspeople, ensuring that traditional techniques are passed down to new generations while adapting to contemporary design sensibilities. Our success will never come at the expense of quality or authenticity.

Our Values

Authenticity

Every product reflects genuine Sri Lankan craftsmanship, ensuring customers receive authentic pieces that honor our cultural heritage.

Community Support

We empower local artisans and communities, providing fair wages and sustaining traditional craft practices for future generations.

Excellence

We maintain unwavering standards of quality in every aspect, from material selection to the final finishing touches.
